Why Captions?

As a high school sign language interpreter, every day I see the struggles of finding good quality videos that also have captions. Closed captions are crucial to our students’ success and good ones are often hard to find. That’s why I made Captain Caption, a website where teachers can access hundreds of videos with closed captions. Captions aren’t just helpful for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community, they are also beneficial to English language learners, students who have a hard time concentrating, and students with learning disabilities.

Enrich your students’ comprehension
and help them with the tools they need to succeed.
About Us

Captain Caption was founded by Monica Szabo, a student at the University of Texas in Austin's Full-Stack Flex Program Coding Bootcamp, who is currently working as a sign language interpreter at a high school in North Austin.
